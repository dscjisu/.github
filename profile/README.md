# 👋 GDG on Campus, JIS University

Student-led Google Developer Group chapter at JIS University, Kolkata. We run workshops, hackathons, study jams, and open-source contribution drives — focused on getting students from *first commit* to *shipping something real*.

Formerly the DSC (Developer Student Club) at JIS University; transitioned to GDG on Campus in 2022.

---

### What we're doing this year

* 📂 **[community-impact-2026](https://github.com/dscjisu/community-impact-2026)** — public log of every event we run this cycle, with photos, recordings, and outcomes
* 🏆 **[study_jams_leaderboard](https://github.com/dscjisu/study_jams_leaderboard)** — Google Cloud Study Jams leaderboard for participants (17★)
* 🎓 **[certifications](https://github.com/dscjisu/certifications)** — certificate records for participants of our workshops and study jams
* 🌸 **[udyamini](https://github.com/dscjisu/udyamini)** — Women's Day initiative spotlighting women in tech

### Evergreen learning resources

* 📚 **[WebDev](https://github.com/dscjisu/WebDev)** — Web Dev Bootcamp curriculum from our 2023 cohort (30★)
* 🗺 **[Roadmaps](https://github.com/dscjisu/Roadmaps)** — curated learning paths for different tech stacks

### Past events archive
